LEVEL 4 — SEBI SCORES (External Escalation)
If your complaint is not resolved satisfactorily after exhausting Tiger Assets' internal escalation process, you may
lodge a grievance on SEBI's online complaint redressal platform:
SCORES Platform

www.scores.sebi.gov.in

SEBI Toll-Free

LEVEL 5 — SMART ODR (Online Dispute Resolution)
Where applicable, disputes in the securities market may also be resolved through SEBI's SMART ODR
mechanism for online conciliation and arbitration. SEBI's grievance framework links SCORES with the ODR
system, providing investors with an efficient, technology-enabled alternative dispute resolution process.
SMART ODR Portal

www.smartodr.in

Applicable For

Disputes in the securities market involving SEBI-registered intermediaries

Process

Online Conciliation → Arbitration (if conciliation fails)

INFORMATION REQUIRED WHILE FILING A COMPLAINT
Please include the following details in your complaint for faster and more efficient resolution:
■ Full name and relationship type (client / prospective client)
■ Registered email address and mobile number
■ Client code / folio number / account reference, if any
■ Nature and description of the complaint
■ Date on which the issue/incident occurred
■ Supporting documents, screenshots, or communication trail
■ Previous reference numbers from prior escalation, if applicable

IMPORTANT NOTE: Complaints relating to investment performance arising solely from normal market movements
may not by themselves constitute service deficiency. However, all client complaints will be reviewed objectively
and responded to in accordance with applicable law and internal grievance procedures.
