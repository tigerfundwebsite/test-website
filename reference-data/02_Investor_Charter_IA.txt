A. VISION AND MISSION STATEMENTS FOR INVESTORS
VISION

MISSION

Invest with knowledge and safety.

Every investor should be able to invest in the right
investment products based on their needs, manage
and monitor them to meet their goals, access reports
and enjoy financial wellness.

B. DETAILS OF BUSINESS TRANSACTED WITH INVESTORS
Tiger Assets, in its capacity as a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser, undertakes the following in accordance with
the SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013 and the Master Circular for Investment Advisers:
• Enter into a written agreement with the client containing all material terms, including scope of services, fees
and charges, conflict of interest disclosures, and other regulatory disclosures.
• Conduct proper and unbiased risk profiling and suitability assessment before rendering personalized
investment advice.
• Ensure required client onboarding, KYC, and related compliance processes are completed in accordance with
applicable law.
• Maintain strict confidentiality of client information, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.
• Employ appropriately qualified and NISM/relevant certified personnel, wherever applicable.
• Conduct required audits and comply with applicable reporting and record-keeping requirements.
• Disclose the status of investor complaints on the website in the SEBI-prescribed format.
• Display registration details, principal office details, grievance contact details, and corresponding SEBI office on
the website.

C. DETAILS OF SERVICES PROVIDED TO INVESTORS
Tiger Assets may provide the following services, subject to the scope of SEBI registration and the executed client
agreement:
Financial Assessment

Evaluation of client's financial situation, investment objectives, time horizon,
and existing portfolio.

Risk Profiling

Structured suitability assessment to determine appropriate investment risk
category.

Asset Allocation Advice

Guidance on asset class allocation and portfolio construction strategy.

Securities
Recommendations

Research-backed advice on specific securities and investment products,
where permitted.

D. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL MECHANISM
In case of any grievance or complaint, an investor should first approach Tiger Assets through the grievance contact
details provided on this website. Tiger Assets shall endeavor to resolve the grievance in a timely and fair manner.
ESCALATION PATH: First contact Tiger Assets (Grievance Officer) → If unresolved, lodge on SEBI SCORES →
Further disputes may be taken through SMART ODR (Online Dispute Resolution).

E. RIGHTS OF INVESTORS
✔ Fair and transparent dealings in all service interactions.
✔ Clear and complete disclosure of fees, risks, and conflicts of interest.
✔ Suitability-based investment advice tailored to individual risk profile and objectives.
✔ Confidential treatment of personal and financial information, subject to law.
✔ Timely response to service requests and grievances.
✔ Access to grievance redressal mechanisms including SCORES and ODR as applicable.
✔ Receive all documents — agreement, fee disclosures, risk profiles — before service commencement.

F. RESPONSIBILITIES OF INVESTORS
■ Read all relevant documents carefully before availing services or acting on advice.
■ Provide complete and accurate information during onboarding, KYC, and suitability assessment.
■ Review all advice, disclosures, agreements, and periodic communications received.
■ Deal only through official Tiger Assets contact points published on the website.
■ Never share login credentials, OTPs, passwords, or sensitive account access details.
■ Inform Tiger Assets promptly of any discrepancy, change in circumstances, or grievance.
■ Understand that all investment decisions carry market risk and no returns are guaranteed.
