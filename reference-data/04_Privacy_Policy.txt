1. WHO WE ARE
This website is operated by Tiger Assets Advisory Private Limited ("Tiger Assets", "we", "our", "us"), a
SEBI-registered Investment Adviser engaged in permitted financial advisory activities. Our Registered Office is
located at [Insert Address]. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal
data when you interact with our website, inquire about our services, or become a client.

2. PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT
Depending on the nature of your interaction with us, we may collect:
• Contact details: Name, email address, mobile number, city, and postal address.
• Identity & KYC data: PAN, date of birth, identity proof, address proof, and related KYC documents required for
onboarding.
• Financial profile: Investment objectives, risk appetite, income range, net worth, bank details, and related
information required for advisory suitability.
• Communications: Service requests, complaint details, and correspondence history.
• Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, pages visited, timestamps,
and cookies / analytics data.

3. PURPOSE OF COLLECTION
We process your personal data only for lawful purposes, including:
• Responding to inquiries and contact form submissions.
• Client onboarding, KYC verification, due diligence, and investment suitability assessment.
• Providing investment advisory or portfolio-related services, as applicable.
• Complying with SEBI, IRDAI, income tax, audit, record-keeping, and anti-fraud obligations.
• Communicating service updates, disclosures, reports, and grievance responses.
• Improving website functionality, security, and overall user experience.
• Detecting unauthorized access, misuse, cyber threats, or suspicious activity.

4. CONSENT AND LEGAL BASIS
Where required under applicable law, Tiger Assets will seek your prior explicit consent before processing your
personal data. Our consent process includes providing clear notice describing: (a) the personal data collected, (b)
the purpose of processing, (c) how you may exercise your rights, and (d) how you may raise a complaint — as
required under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). You may withdraw consent at any time,
subject to legal retention requirements.

5. SHARING OF PERSONAL DATA
Tiger Assets shares personal data only on a strict need-to-know basis and only where appropriate. Sharing may
occur with:
• KYC / CKYC / KRA agencies, custodians, brokers, depositories, banks, RTAs, and technology vendors
engaged for lawful service delivery.

6. DATA RETENTION
We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, including
compliance with contractual, regulatory, tax, audit, and record-retention obligations. Retention periods are
determined by applicable SEBI regulations, income tax law, and internal data governance policies. Upon expiry of
the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. COOKIES AND ANALYTICS
Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality, measure engagement, remember
preferences, and strengthen security. Users may control cookies through browser settings. Disabling certain
cookies may affect website performance. We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal or financial data.

8. DATA SECURITY
Tiger Assets adopts reasonable technical, organizational, and administrative safeguards to protect personal data
against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Security controls include access
restrictions, encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest, and periodic security reviews. However, no method
of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and therefore absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. YOUR RIGHTS
Subject to applicable law (including the DPDP Act 2023), you may have rights to:
• Know what personal data is being processed about you.
• Seek correction, updating, or completion of inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
• Request erasure of data, subject to lawful retention obligations.
• Raise a grievance regarding the handling of your personal data.

10. THIRD-PARTY LINKS
This website may contain links to third-party websites for information or convenience. Tiger Assets is not
